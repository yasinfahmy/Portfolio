About Me

Hello, my name is Yasin Fahmy and I study Business Information Technology at the University of Twente. After graduating high school, I considered taking a gap year and decided to move to Enschede to follow this program. Since then I developed a deep interest in data analysis and strategy.

I understood that it is crucial for businesses to develop a data strategy and support data driven decision making. By discovering patterns and communicating insights to stakeholders, I want to aid companies in making better informed decisions.

As a student from the University of Twente, I have experience in working on IT projects of up to 6 people. Moreover I gathered experience in starting my own projects, including creating a sales team dashboard with Power BI or analyzing and predicting real estate prices.
